## 🧪 The Discrete Equation

The supply decay is brutally simple and deterministic:

$$
S_{n+1} = S_n - f \times T_n
$$

Where:
- $S_n$ = circulating supply **after** transaction $n$  
- $f$ = vault fee fraction (stupidly small: e.g. 0.001%–0.01% or 0.00001–0.0001)  
- $T_n$ = number of tokens moved in transaction $n$

**Key insight**: Because the fee is measured in USD value of the tx, it simplifies to exactly $f\%$ of the tokens being moved. Price cancels out perfectly.

This is **not** a continuous burn. It is a discrete, per-transaction lock that compounds across the entire galaxy of activity.
